Smoked Turkey and Apple Salad

by TastofHome

  • Yield: 4 Servings

  • prep time: 10 MINUTES

  • total time: 20 MINUTES

    • An eye-catching dish, this refreshing salad is a great main course for a summer lunch or light dinner. The dressing's Dijon flavor goes nicely with the turkey, and the apples add crunch. â€”Carolyn Johns, Lacey, Washington

    Ingredients

      For Dressings:

    • 5 tablespoons olive oil
    • 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
    • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
    • 1/2 teaspoon lemon-pepper seasoning

      For Salad:

    • 6 to 8 cups watercress or torn romaine
    • 1 medium carrot, julienned
    • 10 cherry tomatoes, halved
    • 8 ounces sliced deli smoked turkey, cut into strips
    • 4 medium apples, sliced

    Directions

    1. Whisk together first four ingredients.
    2. Place watercress on a platter; top with carrot, tomatoes, turkey and apples. Drizzle with dressing; top with walnuts. Serve immediately.
