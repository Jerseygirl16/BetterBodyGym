About

We are the Better Body Gym, creators of the Better Body Program. We are a new gym looking to provide our members with new experiences, our nationwide gyms provide our members with a safe and comfortable atmosphere to exercise, eat, socialize and relax. We provide the top of the line exercise equipment, a food and nutrition bar, free wifi, and our Better Body Program app, which helps clients maintain their ideal weight and muscle growth outside of the gym, they can go on the app when they aren't at the gym. At Better Body Gym we provide personal trainers that can work with our clients to help them with their goals. We have programs that they can sign-up for and a login site so that they can keep track of all of their progress. We also have a rewards system in which someone can scan their receipts from the grocery stores, supplement stores, apparels and more, and with everything that they scan they can get points which will lead to getting a reward for those points.
